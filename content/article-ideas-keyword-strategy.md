## KEYWORD STRATEGY

### Priority Keyword Patterns
| Pattern | Example | Intent | Difficulty |
|--------|---------|--------|-----------|
| Best [X] for [Y] | Best AI tools for freelancers | Commercial | Medium |
| Top [X] in [Year] | Top AI gadgets 2025 | Commercial | Medium |
| [X] vs [Y] | ChatGPT vs Claude | Commercial | Low-Med |
| How to [X] with AI | How to use AI for marketing | Informational | Low |
| Best [X] under $[N] | Best crypto wallet under $100 | Transactional | Low |
| Is [X] worth it | Is Ledger Nano X worth it | Transactional | Low |
| [X] review | Jasper AI review | Commercial | Medium |
| What is [X] | What is a hardware wallet | Informational | Low |

### Long-Tail Focus
Prioritize long-tail keywords (3–5 words) over head terms. Lower competition, higher conversion intent, easier to rank.

**Target KD:** 0–30 (Semrush/Ahrefs scale)
**Target Monthly Volume:** 500–10,000 searches
**Priority signal:** "buyer words" in query (best, buy, review, vs, alternative, top)
