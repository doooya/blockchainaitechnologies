## 2. DALL-E 3 — Best for Accurate Prompt-Following

DALL-E 3's key strength is instruction precision: it does what you tell it with higher fidelity than Midjourney. When a prompt says "red car on the left, blue car on the right, text reading SALE above," DALL-E 3 delivers that spatial layout reliably. Midjourney would give you something beautiful that may or may not match those specifics.

The ChatGPT integration makes DALL-E 3 the most conversational image generator — you can iterate through multiple refinement prompts in natural conversation, letting the model understand context and continuity rather than treating each prompt as an isolated instruction.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is AI-generated imagery legal to use commercially?**
It depends entirely on which tool and jurisdiction. Adobe Firefly (with indemnification) is the clearest commercial choice. Midjourney, DALL-E 3, and most others grant commercial rights for paid subscribers, but underlying training data IP remains legally unsettled in many countries.

**Can AI image generators replicate a specific person?**
Tools have safety filters preventing realistic generation of specific living people. Character reference features (Midjourney) allow consistent fictional character generation for storytelling and game development.

**Which AI image generator is best for beginners?**
DALL-E 3 via ChatGPT — the conversational interface means you don't need to learn prompting syntax. You can describe what you want naturally and iterate in conversation.

**Does image quality still differ significantly between tools?**
Yes, meaningfully. Midjourney V6 produces the most aesthetically polished outputs by a significant margin for artistic work. DALL-E 3 leads on instruction accuracy. Stable Diffusion with the right model can exceed both for specific use cases but requires significant setup.
